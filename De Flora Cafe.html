About Cafe De Flora

More than just a cafe, it has been an icon of parisian culutre since 1887.
located in the heart of Saint-Germain, it is famous for its classic floral
facade that has hosted history's greatest thinkers. Today, it offers an experience
that blends the luxury of the past with the charm of the present in every cup.
