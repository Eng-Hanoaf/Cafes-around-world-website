About Haraz Cafe

Haraz Cafe represents modern Yemeni coffee culture, the cafe combines traditional Yemeni
flavors with contemporary cafe design. Visitors experience authentic Yemeni coffee
varienties, social gatherings, and a welcoming atmosphere that reflects local heritage
while embracing modern cafe trend. We don't just serve coffee, we preserve a centuries-old
heritage, brewed with modern mastery in warm Yemeni atmosphere.
